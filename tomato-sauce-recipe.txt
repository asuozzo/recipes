Ingredients:
 - 2 cups tomatoes and juices
 - 5 tablespoons butter
 - 1 onion, peeled and cut in half
 - salt

Directions:
  1. Combine tomatoes with juice, butter and onion halves. Add a pinch of salt
  2. Place over medium heat, bring to simmer. Cook uncovered for 45 minutes.
     Stir occasionally, mashing any large pieces with a spoon. Add salt as
     needed.
  3. Discard onion, then toss the sauce with pasta.
